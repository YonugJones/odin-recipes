Sour Creamzees

"Let me embrace thee, sour adversity, for wise men say it is the wisest course"

- William Shakespeare


I bet you thought I had an all liquid diet! HA! Sour cream is a solid! And this treat will become a solid member in your recipe book just like it has become in mine.

Here's what you'll need:

  • 1 cup of cream
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of whole milk
  • toothpicks

Here's what you'll do:

  1. whisk your cream and juice together
  2. let stand for 10 - 15 minutes
  3. add your milk and stir well!
  4. cover the jar with a cheesecloth and let sit for 24 hours
  5. once settled, pour into an ice cub try, cover with film, and poke each sour cream cube with a toothpick
  6. place in a freezer until frozen. Then take out and enjoy!

CAUTION! Stop yourself before eating the full tray. I WOULD NOT recommend it. I've spent a lot of nights in the bathroom because of how GOOD this recipe is :p
