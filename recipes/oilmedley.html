Here's what you'll need:

  • 4 pumps canola oil
  • 3 pumps avocado oil
  • 1.5 pumps extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 squirt of stovetop grease oil (you should have lots of this if you made my previous recipe

Here's what you'll do:

  1. just start pumpking those liquids in!
  2. mix the oils together. It's important you do not add ANY water to this. You know what they say..
  3. After about 15 minutes the oil should have a good froth
  4. IMMEDIATELY pour this oil into your pan that should have been preheating. You want that oil nice and hot
  5. after 30 seconds cut the eat and pour into your serving container of choice (I like cups!)
