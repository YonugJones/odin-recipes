'Dog Water

"We have beaten you to the moon, but you have beaten us in sausage making."

- Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev


Don't let the soviets get a hold of this recipe! This tasty treat will take you to the moon and back. The best part is, you probably already have most of the ingredients at home!

Here's what you'll need:

  • 36 hot dogs
  • 4 gallons of water (dasani is my preferred brand!)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cloves of garlic

Here's what you'll do:

  1. Boil the water
  2. add your hot dogs, salt, and garlic to the water (all at once!)
  3. boil the dogs until you can't wait no more cuz you're sooo excited to drink that sweet dog nectar
  4. Discard the dogs (OR if you're on a budget, put them in a resealable bag and freeze them for another round, probably later tonight ;)
  5. DRINK!

Easy right?! Let me know what you think! If you like them half as much as I do, I know you'll have this in regular rotation for you and your family.
