Hot Dog Water

"We have beaten you to the moon, but you have beaten us in sausage making." - Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev

Don't let the soviets get a hold of this recipe!

This tasty treat will take you to the moon and back. The best part is, you probably already have most the ingredients at home!

Here's what you'll need:

  • 36 hot dogs
  • 4 gallongs of water (dasani is our preferred brand!)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cloves of garlic
